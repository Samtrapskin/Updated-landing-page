Who ?

My name is Sam. I'm a Web Developer and Designer based in the Twin Cities in Minnesota. A few years go I was inspired to change careers with a goal of discovering new and challenging opportunities within Software and Web Development. I've gained experience in a lot of great opportunities. I currently work for Stratasys. One of the world's biggest 3D Printing manufacturers as a Web and Digital Marketing specialist.

Throughout my life, I've valued my curiosity with technology and have always loved learning new skills. I'm in the process of learning User Experience / User Interface Design. I've also completed a class in Graphic Design both from the Minneapolis College of Art and Design to add to my foundation.

Hey there, I'm Sam Trapskin

I’m a web designer, UX enthusiast, and digital marketing strategist who thrives on creating seamless, engaging online experiences. With a knack for blending design, strategy, and a touch of psychology, I make websites work smarter—not harder.

I’m currently at St. Catherine University, wearing multiple hats in web design, web development, digital marketing, UX, and content management. Whether optimizing user journeys, refining SEO, or streamlining content, I’m always looking for new ways to push the boundaries of what’s possible.

I didn’t just stumble into this field—I jumped in, fueled by curiosity and a love for problem-solving. Whether it’s optimizing user journeys, fine-tuning SEO, or learning the latest tech, I’m always pushing the boundaries of what’s possible.

When I’m not geeking out over web design and marketing trends, you’ll find me experimenting with new tools, refining my craft, and making the internet a little better—one pixel at a time.

If you have an opportunity in mind or just want to collaborate, let’s connect and create something great together!

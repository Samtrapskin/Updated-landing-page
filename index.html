Who I Am

My name is Sam. I'm a Web Developer and Designer based in the Twin Cities in Minnesota. A few years go I was inspired to change careers with a goal of discovering new and challenging opportunities within Software Development.

I've always been curious about technology and love learning new skills. I'm in the process of learning User Experience / User Interface Design. I've also completed a class in Graphic Design both from the Minneapolis College of Art and Design to add to my foundation.
